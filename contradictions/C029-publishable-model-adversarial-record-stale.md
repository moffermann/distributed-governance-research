# C029 - Publishable Model Carries a Stale Adversarial Record

## Status

Open — pending author review.

## Contradiction

Side A — `docs/101_CORE_V0_MINIMAL_PUBLISHABLE_MODEL.md` (the external-facing companion):

> **Adversarially.** Thirty-three attack briefs grounded in the political science and economics literature (`docs/101_CORE_V0_MINIMAL_PUBLISHABLE_MODEL.md`, "How this model has been tested" section)
> resolutions `docs/67`–`docs/100` (same section)
> "...it is recorded as an open attack on the model (a wikilink to the A035 attack file)" and "...doctrinally open, likewise recorded (a wikilink to the A034 attack file)" (`docs/101_CORE_V0_MINIMAL_PUBLISHABLE_MODEL.md:784`)

Side B — the current corpus and the manuscript:

> Thirty-five attacks, all resolved, resolutions `docs/67`–`docs/103` (`drafts/paper.md:37,95`, `defenses/README.md:95-96`); A034 resolved in `docs/102`, A035 resolved in `docs/103`.

## Why they cannot both be true

The companion tells an external reader the record has 33 attacks ending at docs/100 with A034/A035 open; the manuscript it accompanies says 35, ending at docs/103, all resolved. A reviewer reading both documents — the intended reading path — hits the discrepancy immediately.

## Author-intent side

Side B. The A034/A035 resolutions were accepted with the author's own arguments at their core.

## Proposed treatment

Update the "Adversarially" bullet to thirty-five, its resolution range to `docs/67`–`docs/103`, and rewrite the line 784 passage: the two costs remain honestly declared, but they are now *resolved* — the Administrative Capacity Declaration (`docs/103`) and the allocation-act characterization record (`docs/102`) — with their residual limits (no empirical staffing number until a pilot; doctrine external). Wording change only; the honest-costs framing stays.

## Severity

High — external-facing companion; contradicts the manuscript on the method's headline numbers.
