# Panel opinion — INSTITUTIONAL-DESIGN seat (pro-Core-v0)

Reviewer stance: Core v0's edge is **institutional** — separation of the select/execute/judge
functions, observability, anti-capture, and the **salience-gated harm-myopia** mechanism (central
oversight reaches the visible few; the low-visibility long tail is where it goes blind). The paper's
real contribution is that mechanism, made criticizable and pilotable. Numbers are *support*, not the
headline. Form-only review of `drafts/paper.md` (+ ES mirror `drafts/paper.es.md`); no substance change.

Scope reviewed: abstract (L9-16), §6 findings + governing test (L509-620), the v1.14 extension
(EN L622-656 / ES L682-715), "What survives" (L1088-1102), appendix "E4 calibration targets"
(L1469-1493).

## (B) POSITION on the restructure — SUPPORT, with a mechanism-first framing

I support headlining the **probable** scenario in the body and relocating the **full** four-scenario
comparative to a dedicated appendix — *on one condition*: the body must **lead with the mechanism and
carry the honest bracket**, not lead with a single favorable number.

Why this is honest, not spin:
1. **The mechanism is the load-bearing claim.** The probable scenario is one point on a declared
   robustness map; the *mechanism* (credit-vs-coverage + salience-gated harm-myopia) is what generalizes.
   Leading with the mechanism and using the probable number as an illustration is the accurate altitude —
   it is *less* over-claiming than the current inline table, which invites the reader to treat five rows
   as five calibrated estimates.
2. **No information loss.** Everything currently at L622-656 (four-scenario table, the diagnostic
   harm-myopia-only contrast, the sequential 40%/60% decomposition, the t≈0.57 frontier, the joint-normal
   parity theorem, the four honest limits) moves *verbatim in substance* into an expanded E4 appendix. The
   body keeps a live pointer.
3. **The current body paragraph is over-long for its altitude.** ~35 lines carrying five point estimates,
   a path-dependent decomposition, and a theorem sits above the reader's need at that point in §6. That is
   a *form* problem the restructure fixes.

The restructure is an improvement in honesty **only if** my red lines below hold. If the central's winning
endpoint or the NO-GO anchor leave the body, I flip to OPPOSE — that would be the spin the author disavows.
