## Not mapped (declared boundaries)

- **Planning signal mixes** stay at the planning-vector calibration (`PLANNING_CALIBRATION_FROM_EXPERIMENT_2.md` governs them). The behavioral model's emergent distributed correlation (~0.97) is an honest-signals upper bound, not a calibration; injecting it would smuggle the docs/87 elicitation problem out of existence.
- **Fiscalizer composition** (competent/weak/capturable): the behavioral model does not model capture; the adversarial defaults stay.
- **Attacks** remain the adversarial experiment's own scenario choices.
- **Trust dynamics** do not cross the bridge in v1: the adversarial engine has no trust state; the behavioral feedback loop is already reflected in the equilibrium shares being mapped.
- **Scale**: 1,000 behavioral citizens inform shares applied to 10,000 adversarial citizens â€” shares are scale-free; declared assumption of the bridge.
