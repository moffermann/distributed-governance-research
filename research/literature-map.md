## Initial observation

The specific combination developed in the conversation does not appear to be captured by a single existing frame:

- functional decomposition of state institutions;
- distribution criteria based on the nature of the function;
- distributed resource allocation;
- institutional competition and auditability;
- distributed trust as a general architecture;
- AI as coordinator or personal guide, not central sovereign;
- selective centralization for defense and coercive security.

## Research task

Find papers that support, contradict, or partially anticipate each component.
